My Lasagna Recipe

Description

I am a certified pasta lover and one of my favorite types is lasagna. Here is my personal recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 packages of storebought lasagna noodles (whole grain; amount depends on pan depth)
  • 2-3 jars of storebought pasta sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cream cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Spoon a thin layer of pasta sauce into the bottom of a rectangular pan.
  2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, covering as much of the pan as possible.
  3. Add another layer of pasta sauce overtop.
  4. Add various cheeses to your liking.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until pan is full.
  6. Top with a final layer of cheese and cover with a generous helping of parmesan cheese.
