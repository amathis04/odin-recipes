My Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for authentic Spanish flan. I am a six year student of the Spanish language and love learning about recipes from Latin America and other Spanish-speaking countries! This is one of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • Three eggs
  • One egg yolk
  • 370ml condensed milk
  • 370ml whole milk
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 3tbsp caster sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add sugar to a small saucepan over medium heat. Heat until the sugar is dark caramel. Pour in a little water at a time and mix until the caramel is homogenised. Pour the caramel into a flan mould and coat the sides. Set aside.
  2. Whisk the eggs and yolk in a bowl and mix in the condensed milk, whole milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the mixture from the bowl into the flan mould carefully.
  4. Place the mould carefully into a pot of water with a lid on it and cook at a low temperature for 30 minutes or until the flan is set.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
