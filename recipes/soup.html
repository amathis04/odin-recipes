Curried Lentil Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

This looks incredible. Here's a recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2tbsp Coconut Oil
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 3tbsp Ginger
  • 1tbsp Curry Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 28oz Roasted Diced Tomatoes
  • 1cup Green Lentils
  • 2.5cups Water
  • 14oz Coconut Milk
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1/2cup Cilantro
  • Fresh Lime Juine
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, and red pepper flakes together until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add tomatoes, lentils, water, coconut milk, salt, and pepper. Bring to boil then simmer until lentils are tender, or 25 to 35 minutes.
  3. Add cilanto and lime juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
